Chicken Noodle Soup

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Hearty homemade Chicken Noodle Soup (made with love).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 4 (1.5 oz) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can vegetable broth
  • 1/2 lb chopped chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cup egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt butter in large pot over medium heat.
  2. Cook onion and celery in butter for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in chicken and vegetable broth.
  4. Stir in chicken, noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and, pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil.
  6. Simmer 20 minutes before serving.
